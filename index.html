What Is CO—WORKING?

Internal Overview

CO—WORKING is a New York-based creative collective operating somewhere between:

  • an art project,
  • a consultancy,
  • a media company,
  • an experiential events brand,
  • and a fictional corporation.

It exists as both a real business and an ongoing piece of worldbuilding.

The company explores

  • freelance culture,
  • startup mythology,
  • corporate aesthetics,
  • productivity theater,
  • internet identity,
  • modern creative labor.

The tone oscillates between

satire&sincerity corporate&underground nostalgia&dystopia functionality&absurdity
"An immersive creative consultancy disguised as a corporate cult."

DEPT. 005

Panel Discussions

An event series disguised as corporate meetings

You're not "attending a party." You're "joining a mandatory quarterly alignment session."

DJs perform from conference rooms, abandoned offices, cubicle farms, midtown towers, hotel business centers. Attendees receive calendar invites, onboarding documents, NDAs, fake internal memos, and employee credentials.

Day Pass

Free "permission slip" to leave work for happy hour — dramatizes the thesis at near-zero cost. Recurring acquisition format.

Venue: rooftop bar, TBD — sunset-facing, happy hour hours.

Beach Edition

Real panel/Q&A staged in swimsuits at a beach DJ/concessions area. High shareability, low production build.

Venue: Jacob Riis Beach — outreach pending.

Non-Alc Meeting

Daytime, alcohol-free networking format. Opens a Friday-morning slot. Breakfast-meeting register.

Venue: Index Space (Chinatown / Greenpoint) or N Between.
